Personal Logo Design


Goal

The goal of this graphic design project was to create a professional logo which I can use in my career as a UX designer.


Software

Sketchbook  
Adobe Illustrator  


The Process

The following three images display my initial thumbnail sketches. This was the brainstorming phase. I decided to focus on my initials as I do not have a business name, so my own name will be used for my brand. I also feel that these two initials, J and P, can be paired nicely in a logo.




The following four images contain my favourite sketches from the above thumbnail sketches. I decided narrowing these sketches down to a few would make more sense for when I begin experimenting digitally. You can view my semi-comps created in Adobe Illustrator, based on the below four sketches.




The following colour palette contains the five colours I will use to distinguish my brand. The first two colours are the primary colours and the following three are the secondary colours.

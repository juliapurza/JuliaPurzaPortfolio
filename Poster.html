UX Design Recruitment Poster


Goal

The goal of this practical test was to create a recruitment poster that could possibly be used by Wilfrid Laurier University during open houses and other events to attract future students to the User Experience Design program. The criteria was to stick to Laurier's brand standards and colour scheme, and create a design that features original graphics.

Final Result

After looking at inspiration, researching Laurier colours and logos, and designing some rough drafts, this is the final result.

I incorporated Laurier's main colour palette, making sure that the poster officially represents the university. I digitally designed some simple icons that represent main aspects of user experience design, including graphic design, collaboration, research, and interface. I also used a motto that is fairly popular in the UX world that I feel perfectly represents this program. We as UX students and designers heavily focus on the design process, uncovering the issues and possible solitions for clients.

Next Steps

My group and I have connected with Laurier LaunchPad who will connect us to Brantford businesses who might want to partner with us and introduce us to grants that we can make use of to implement our QR code concept. This presentation will be used to introduce the concept to businesses who might be interested in working with us. My group and I are meeting on a regular basis to make sure we are all on the same page and to coninue updating and practicing our ideas.

Our goal remains to connect locals and tourists to local businesses in Brantford, encouraging individuals to discover new businesses as well as bringing customers and technology to these businesses.

WHO AM I?


Hi there! My name is Julia Purza and I am a UX design student at Wilfrid Laurier University in Brantford, Ontario.

Since I can remember, I loved drawing, painting, and making use of any traditional media. I went to Conestoga College for a program called Design Foundations which introduced me to traditional art, graphic design and web design. This is where I first heard about UX!

I decided to apply to Laurier’s new UX program and here I am. I am currently a third year UX student studying at Laurier's Brantford campus. I am also working as a UX designer for the Waterloo Region Suicide Prevention Council. My skills range from traditional art to graphic design to UX and web design. In addition, I used my beginner coding skills to code this website from scratch using HTML5, CSS, and PHP in Visual Studio Code and I am slowly working my way to expanding these skills.

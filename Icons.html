Icon Design


Goal

The goal of this practical test was to create any icon design that meets the universal icon standards and dimensions. This included making sure there is enough detail, but not too much, as well as making sure the dimensions are exact.


Software

Sketchbook.  
Adobe Illustrator.  


Inspiration and Sketches

The first image portrays my initial sketches while I was still deciding which theme to choose. I find thumbnails to be an effective way to get out as many ideas as possible for a more successful outcome. Once I chose to create muay thai icons, you can see in the second photo that I used a collection of online images as inspiration.


    


You can view my rough drafts which I sketched out before moving on to designing with Adobe Illustrator. Sketching out ideas before moving on to digital design is important because it allows for more design freedom, resulting in more complex ideas. Once I obtained my final design through sketch, I scanned it, pasted it into Illustrator and used it as an effective template to digitally design over.

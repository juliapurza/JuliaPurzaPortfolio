ABOUT ME


Hi there! My name is Iulia Purza (with an i, not L), but you can call me Julia. Currently, I am a second-year UX (user experience) design major at Wilfrid Laurier University in Brantford, ON.

I previously attended Conestoga College in Kitchener, ON where I was enrolled in the Design Foundations program. I acquired valuable fine arts, communications, and design skills. I experimented with a large number of fine arts mediums and worked with software such as Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, and PhotoShop where I gained graphic design skills. After gaining valuable fine arts and graphic design skills, I decided to take it to another level and study UX design.
